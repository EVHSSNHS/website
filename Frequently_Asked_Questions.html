Frequently Asked Questions



Do I get an official transcript, unofficial transcript, or a grad inventory from the registrar’s office?

You should obtain a grad inventory instead. I know we wanted you guys to obtain a transcript from the registrar; however, they wanted to save resources so a grad inventory will do.

Do I have to be taking an AP Science in order to apply?

You must provide proof that you applied for the AP science course only IF YOU DIDN’T get the AP science course.

Do I put my name on the application?

No, PLEASE DO NOT PUT YOUR NAME ON THE APPLICATION. We stress the importance of this because we want all apps to be reviewed without knowing who the app belongs to. Only put your ID and grade number on the “header” of the word document. We automatically reject anyone who fails to follow this rule. Thank you!

Do our science related awards/activities have to be recent?

You may only include science related awards/activities that occurred during high school (so from the beginning of 9th grade and beyond.)

How do I create my own chapter?

Are you interested in creating a new chapter for your own school? Check out the SNHS FAQ page
